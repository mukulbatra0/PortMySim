Our Story

PortMySim was born from a simple observation: mobile number porting should be easier and more convenient. Our founders experienced firsthand the challenges of timing port requests with plan expiry dates and dealing with service interruptions.

Founded in 2023, we set out to create a solution that would make the porting process seamless and stress-free. Today, we're proud to help thousands of users transition between mobile operators without any hassle.
