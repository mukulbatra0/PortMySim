Porting Process Guide

Submit Porting Request

Fill out the form with your current mobile number, provider, and plan end date.

SMS Notification Date

You'll receive an SMS with your UPC code 3 working days before your plan ends (or 5 working days for Jammu & Kashmir users).

Note: Working days exclude Sundays and telecom company holidays.

Generate UPC Code

On the SMS notification date, send an SMS with "PORT" to 1900 from your current number to receive your UPC code.

Visit New Provider

Visit your new service provider's store with your UPC code, ID proof, and address proof. They'll verify and process your request.

Porting Completion

Your number will be ported within 3-5 days. During the transition, you might experience a brief service interruption (usually less than 4 hours).

Our system automatically calculates the optimal SMS notification date based on your plan's end date and telecom circle rules. For Jammu & Kashmir users, we schedule notifications 5 working days before plan expiry instead of the standard 3 days.
