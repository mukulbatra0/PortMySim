Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about the porting process

How long does the porting process take?
The porting process typically takes 3-5 working days. However, with PortXpress's smart scheduling, we ensure your service transition is seamless by initiating the process at the optimal time.
Will I lose my data during porting?
No, you won't lose any data stored on your phone. However, any unused balance or data pack with your current operator will not be transferred to the new operator.
Can I cancel my porting request?
Yes, you can cancel your porting request within 24 hours of submission. After that, the process cannot be reversed until completion.
What happens if porting fails?
If porting fails, your number will remain with your current operator. Our support team will help you understand the reason for failure and guide you through the next steps.
